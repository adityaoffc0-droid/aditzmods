Minecraft Bedrock Shader

Newb - GoldenMax

Compatibility Note

Only works with MB Loader

Newb - GoldenXtra is an enhanced vanilla shader designed to improve the visual quality of Minecraft while preserving the original gameplay experience. It delivers warm golden lighting, vibrant colors, improved sky rendering, realistic atmospheric fog, smoother shadows, and refined environmental effects without changing the classic Minecraft style.

This shader focuses on performance and compatibility, making it suitable for Android devices running Minecraft Bedrock through MB Loader. Every visual enhancement has been carefully balanced to provide a beautiful and immersive world while maintaining stable gameplay.

Golden LightingWarm golden sunrise and sunset lighting.
Vibrant ColorsEnhanced daylight with vibrant yet natural colors.
Night AtmosphereImproved night atmosphere with softer moonlight.
Sky & CloudsBeautiful gradients and realistic cloud options.
Dynamic FogDynamic fog with optional fog-free subpacks.
Smooth AnimationSmooth waving leaves, grass, and crops.
Water EffectsEnhanced water appearance and reflections.
Cave AmbienceBetter cave ambience and environmental lighting.
Chunk LoadingOptional chunk loading animation.
Cloud StylesRealistic, Rounded, and Box Cloud styles.
Performance FriendlyOptimized for a wide range of Android devices.
Modular SubpacksCustomize your visual experience easily.
Vanilla FaithfulPreserves original vanilla look while making the world feel richer and alive.

Whether you're exploring vast landscapes, building impressive creations, or surviving through the night, Newb - GoldenXtra delivers a visually stunning experience without sacrificing Minecraft's iconic aesthetic.
